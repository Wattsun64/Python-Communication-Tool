What is Happening?

On Saturday, 8 July 2017, the Identity and Access Management team will implement identity and access management information within the Microsoft Azure cloud service. This activity will provide Azure with user and group information from the University of Michigan Active Directory UMROOT domain. User and group data population will begin and continue until complete.
